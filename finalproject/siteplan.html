Manos de Seda: Site Plan

Site Name: Manos de Seda Spa

The massages done in "Manos de Seda" or Silk Hands in English end by reconnecting you with yourself and relaxing you at the end of the day, feeling as if your whole body were dressed in silk clothing.

Site Purpose:

The website is intended to create an immediate local presence, so that people interested in the services can see a bit of the history, the services provided, the prices and can schedule an appointment.

Scenarios

The following scenarios represent common questions from our target audience:

  • Scenario 1: "Do you have therapists who specialize in therapeutic treatments?"
  • Scenario 2: "How can I book an appointment online, and what are your current availability and pricing options?"
  • Scenario 3: "Do you offer corporate services?"
